# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **SynthX** ("the Bot", "we", "our") collects, uses, and stores data when you interact with it on Discord.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

SynthX collects and stores the following data to provide its features:

### User Data
- **User IDs** — used for moderation records, leveling, AFK status, warnings, custom roles, invite tracking, profiles, and premium status.
- **Message content** — stored temporarily for AI conversation history (up to the configured history limit) and used for automod filtering. Message content is not stored permanently beyond its functional purpose.
- **Username and avatar** — accessed in real-time from Discord's API for display purposes (e.g. welcome cards, user info commands). Not stored independently.

### Server (Guild) Data
- **Guild IDs** — used to associate all server-specific settings and configurations.
- **Channel and Role IDs** — stored as part of server configuration (e.g. logging channels, welcome channels, auto-role setups, reaction roles).
- **Invite data** — tracked per-guild for the invite tracking feature (inviter IDs, invite codes, use counts).
- **Audit events** — logged per-guild when logging features are enabled (bans, kicks, message deletions, etc.).

### Interaction Data
- **Command usage** — recorded to support features such as giveaways, tickets, leveling, and games.
- **Voice activity** — tracked per-session for voice master features.
- **Boost data** — stored for booster reward tracking.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Data collected by SynthX is used exclusively to operate the Bot's features, including:

- Moderation tools (bans, kicks, timeouts, warnings, jail, anti-nuke)
- Server automation (auto-role, auto-react, auto-responder, welcome/goodbye messages)
- Leveling and XP systems
- Giveaways and ticket systems
- Logging and audit trails
- AI chatbot conversations
- Games and entertainment features
- Invite tracking and vanity URL management

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose outside of operating the Bot.

---

## 3. Data Retention

- **Server configuration data** is retained for as long as the Bot remains in your server.
- **User-specific data** (warnings, levels, profiles) is retained until manually cleared by a server administrator or until the Bot is removed from the server.
- **AI conversation history** is stored only up to the configured history limit and is overwritten as new messages are added.
- When the Bot is removed from a server, server-specific data may be deleted automatically or upon request.

---

## 4. Data Access and Deletion

- **Server administrators** can clear server-specific data (configurations, warnings, logs) using the Bot's built-in commands.
- **Users** can request deletion of their personal data (e.g. profiles, AFK records, level data) by contacting us via the support server.
- To request full data deletion, join our support server: **https://discord.gg/WKX5HHPmWz**

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

SynthX may interact with third-party services to provide certain features:

- **OpenAI API** — used to power the AI chatbot. Messages sent to the AI feature may be processed by OpenAI in accordance with their [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy).
- **Prodia API** — used for AI image generation. Prompts submitted to this feature are processed by Prodia.
- **Discord API** — all data access occurs through Discord's API and is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

---

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access. Data is stored in local SQLite databases. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

SynthX is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a minor's data has been collected, please contact us immediately.
